Description

Application for multichannel e-commerce platform to automatically manage, list, and sell products to the most popular online marketplaces such as eBay and Amazon. The app allows hooking up Online shopping/dropshipping vendors that support and provides daily/regular inventory updates via one of the most popular application programming interface (API or web API) such as RESTful, FTP, sFTP, HTTP, etc. The products are listed and managed on an e-Commerce site via a feed-based selling system. The application currently supports the following vendors WPS, Helmet House, and Part Unlimited and manages listings on eBay and Amazon. The app allows tools for optimizing product listing and selling such as category suggestions, aspects/features requirements, Kit(ing) and grouping, and comparing products to other sellers' live listings.
